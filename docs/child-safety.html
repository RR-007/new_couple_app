UsQuest — Child Safety Standards

Effective Date: February 27, 2026

UsQuest is committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users. We have a zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and any content that promotes, depicts, or facilitates harm to minors.

1. Age Requirement

UsQuest is intended for users aged 18 years and older. We do not knowingly allow individuals under the age of 18 to create accounts or use the platform.

2. Prohibited Content

The following content and behavior are strictly prohibited:

  • Any form of child sexual abuse material (CSAM).
  • Sexual content involving minors.
  • Grooming, exploitation, or predatory behavior toward minors.
  • Attempts to share, solicit, or distribute illegal content.

3. User Reporting & Moderation

  • Users can report inappropriate content or behavior directly within the app.
  • We review all reports and take appropriate action, including account suspension or permanent removal.
  • Accounts found violating these standards are immediately terminated.

4. Enforcement & Cooperation

If we become aware of credible evidence of child exploitation or illegal activity, we reserve the right to:

  • Remove offending content immediately.
  • Suspend or permanently ban involved accounts.
  • Cooperate with law enforcement authorities where required by law.

5. Preventative Measures

  • Access is restricted to adult users only (18+).
  • Account authentication is required to access app features.
  • Content is shared only between linked partners.
