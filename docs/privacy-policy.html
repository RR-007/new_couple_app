UsQuest — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 27, 2026

UsQuest ("we", "our", "us") is a personal relationship app designed for couples to share lists, memories, photos, and activities together. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address and password — Used for authentication via Firebase Authentication.
  • Display name — Shown to your partner within the app.
  • Partner linking code — A unique code used to connect two accounts as a couple.

User-Generated Content

  • Photos and videos — Uploaded for features like Pic of the Day, Diary, Bingo, and Quests. Stored via Cloudinary (a third-party image hosting service).
  • Text content — Love Notes, diary entries, list items, recipes, watchlist entries, and quest/bingo data. Stored in Google Firebase Firestore.
  • Calendar events — Created within the app or synced via Google Calendar (with your explicit OAuth consent).

Device & Technical Data

  • Push notification tokens — Used to deliver notifications via Expo Push Notification service.
  • Device type and OS version — Collected automatically for app compatibility.

Google Calendar Data (Optional)

  • If you choose to connect your Google Calendar, we access your calendar events to display them in the app's shared calendar view.
  • We do not store your Google Calendar data on our servers — it is fetched in real-time.
  • You can disconnect Google Calendar at any time from the app's Settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core app functionality (shared lists, photos, notes, quests, etc.).
  • To sync data in real-time between you and your partner.
  • To send push notifications (new notes, quest assignments, event reminders).
  • To display your Google Calendar events (only if you opted in).

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics profiling, or sell it to third parties.

3. Data Sharing

Your data is shared only with:

  • Your linked partner — This is the core purpose of the app.
  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication and database hosting.
  • Cloudinary — Photo and video storage.
  • Expo — Push notification delivery.
  • Google Calendar API — Only if you connect your calendar.

We do not share data with any other third parties.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore) with security rules that restrict access to authenticated, linked couples only.
  • Photos are stored in Cloudinary with unique, non-guessable URLs.
  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption.
  • Passwords are handled by Firebase Authentication and are never stored in plain text.

5. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have the right to:

  • Access all your data within the app at any time.
  • Delete individual items (notes, photos, list items, etc.) at any time.
  • Request full account deletion — Contact us (see below) and we will delete all your data from our systems within 30 days.
  • Disconnect Google Calendar at any time from Settings.

6. Children's Privacy

UsQuest is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
