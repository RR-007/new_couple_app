Request Account Deletion

⚠️ Important: This action is irreversible. All your data will be permanently deleted.

What happens when you request deletion:

  • Your UsQuest account will be permanently deleted
  • All your shared lists, notes, diary entries, and photos will be removed
  • Your link with your partner will be disconnected
  • All quest progress and bingo data will be lost
Note: If you only want to remove specific items (photos, notes, lists), you can do that directly in the app instead of deleting your entire account.

How to request account deletion:

Email us at rohitrajeev02@gmail.com with:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your display name in the app
  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"

We will process your request within 30 days and send you a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.
